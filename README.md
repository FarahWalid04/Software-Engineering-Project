# 🔐 Kenzee Locksmith Digital Service & Dispatch System

A Software Engineering project that designs a modern digital platform for managing locksmith service requests, technician dispatching, customer tracking, and business administration. The system replaces manual booking and dispatch processes with an automated, reliable, and user-friendly web solution.

---

## 📖 Project Overview

Kenzee Locksmith traditionally relies on phone calls and manual record-keeping to manage customer requests. These processes often result in:

- Delayed emergency response times
- Manual technician assignment
- No real-time technician tracking
- Lack of centralized customer records
- Difficult business management and scaling

This project proposes a **Digital Service & Dispatch System** that automates service requests, technician assignment, customer communication, and reporting.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

- Automate service booking and technician dispatch
- Provide real-time service tracking
- Improve customer experience and transparency
- Centralize customer and service records
- Support secure online payments
- Deliver reliable 24/7 emergency locksmith services

## 🔄 System Workflow

1. Customer registers or logs in.
2. Selects a locksmith service category.
3. Verifies service location.
4. Submits a booking request.
5. System validates the request.
6. Technician is automatically dispatched.
7. Customer receives SMS updates.
8. Technician completes the service.
9. Customer makes payment.
10. Customer rates and reviews the service.
